Search for today's news in Uttar Pradesh, India using the Chrome/Playwright browser.

Task details:
1. Open a headless Chrome browser and navigate to a search engine (Google or similar)
2. Search for "Uttar Pradesh news today" or similar query
3. Visit top 3-5 relevant news sources from the search results
4. Extract key headlines and summaries of current news stories
5. Organize findings into a clear report with:
   - Major headlines
   - Brief descriptions of key stories
   - Source URLs
   - Timestamp of when news was published (if available)
6. Save the compiled news report to a markdown file in the logs directory

Focus on credible news sources and cover a variety of topics (politics, economy, social issues, etc.).
